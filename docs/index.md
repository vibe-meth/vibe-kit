## FAQ

**Q: Is VibeCoder right for my project?**
A: If you prefer flow and intuition over exhaustive planning, yes. See [VibeCoder vs Spec-Kit](./vibe-vs-spec.md).

**Q: Do I need all 6 commands?**
A: No. The 3 core commands (/plan, /tasks, /execute) are enough. The 3 optional commands are advisory.

**Q: Can I use VibeCoder with any AI agent?**
A: Yes! VibeCoder works with Claude, Copilot, Cursor, Amp, Gemini, and 13+ others.

**Q: How is confidence scoring different from task estimates?**
A: Confidence is about your trust in the plan's direction, not task duration. 8/10 confident means proceed; 4/10 means clarify.

**Q: What if my confidence drops mid-execution?**
A: Use /vibekit.insight to analyze what changed. Then pivot‚Äîno gates, no approval needed.
